## VIII. Post-Flood Context

These laws are the genome of the V2 organism. V1 was built empirically (127 repos, ad-hoc governance). The flood washed it away (SPEC-019 liquid constitutional order). The laws written in this session are the seeds planted into clean soil. The organism has not yet regrown — the `organvm` GitHub org (IRF-LIQ-001), the distillation pipeline (IRF-LIQ-002), and the excretory system (IRF-LIQ-003) are the regrowth mechanisms. These laws are what those mechanisms will express.

LIM-II (Criticality) applies to the genome itself: the acorn must be complex enough to grow a tree but not so complex it can't germinate. LIM-III (Compression) governs the genome's evolution: as V2 grows, redundant laws compress into fewer, more powerful principles. The final constitution should be shorter than this one.
